A bit about me

Hello my name is Kuno. I am a dutch Engineer that was raised in the North east of England. I don't like showing off so I will keep this short:

I have always been into engineering since a very young age. By the time I was five I was always asking my parents about the fine detail of how things work, they were quickly finding it hard to answer my questions. They bought me a book called "The Way Things Work" (I still have it to this day, it's very tatty now) that showed me how many common objects operated from coal powerplants to air horns. Let's just say that my curiosity hasn't stopped. I am still amazed at the engineering achievements that humanity has accomplised and the potential of the future that I am excited to be part of. Since then I have spent my childhood and teenage years either designing, building and researching engineering concepts or outside having adventures on mountain bikes and Kayaks.
